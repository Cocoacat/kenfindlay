About Ken                          

Ken Findlay is a college lecturer with over 22   years of experience in adventure education. His passion is to promote the benefits of outdoor exploration and to make it available to all. As an experienced mountaineer he has climbed over 200 mountains worldwide and embarked upon 13 expeditions. He is a member of The British Mountaineering Council (BMC), The Alpine Club (AC), The Fell and Rock Climbing Club (FRCC) and The Leeds Mountaineering Club (LMC). He is also a keen runner and has clocked up over 1,000 miles in marathons, which includes The Karrimor International Mountain Marathon. More

 






  • 1991 British Shimshal Glacier Expedition ( Pakistan ) 'A group of virgins'
