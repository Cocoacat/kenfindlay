WALKS

Q: What standard of fitness do I need to have?
A: It does not matter, from somebody who has never walked before to more experienced walkers, we cater for all abilities.
Q  I have never done any walking before. What if I can’t manage the walking route?
A: Every enquirer will be assessed on their previous experience and level of fitness before booking a walk with KenTrek. We are always happy to discuss any questions you have.
Q:How difficult are your walks?
A: Our walks are graded and covers three main levels - Easy, Moderate and Difficult.
Q: Are dogs allowed on walks?
A: : No dogs are not allowed.
Q: What equipment do I need?
A: No equipment is needed, if we do use compasses, then KenTrek will supply them. You are most welcome to bring your own maps and compasses if you wish.
Q: What clothing do I need?
A: You will need to bring a waterproof jacket and trousers, warm clothing, hat, gloves and sensible footwear.
Q: What food/drink do I need on the walk?
A:: All attendees must bring their own food and drink.
Q:  Can you supply any clothing or equipment needed for the walks?
A: We don’t have a supply of clothing or equipment but we could supply some of the above in special circumstances.
Q: Are maps included in the price?
A:Maps are not included in the price.
Q: What if the weather is a problem?
 A:We always have a bad weather alternative which we will discuss with you.
Q:  Do you offer any travel advice/links for transport to the location?
A: We offer travel advice but we meet all our attendees at the start of the walk.

TRAINING PACKAGES

Q: What is the standard length of one of your training packages?
A: There is no standard length for the training packages. They range from one hour to a whole day.
Q:  How much previous experience do I need for any of the training?
A: No previous experience necessary.
Q: What is the maximum number of people you can accommodate on a course of training?
 A:It would depend on the training packages. For the walks, the ratio would be 1:8.
Q: Is there a minimum age limit?
A: No age limit but anyone under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
Q: Does the price include course materials?
A:Yes it does. All course materials are provided free.
Q: What clothing/equipment do I need?
A: It would depend on the training package you have chosen.
Q: Can you supply any clothing or equipment needed?
A: Again this would depend on the training package.
Q: How will the course be assessed?
A: There are no assessments.
Q: If the weather is bad, will the course still go ahead?
A: Yes as most of the training packages will be indoors.
